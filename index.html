2

3

4

Project Goal: Decentralization

Our long-term vision is to make Commentator a fully decentralized public commenting system that cannot be controlled or censored by any single entity.

Decentralized Storage: Store comment data on IPFS or Arweave using tools like web3.storage or Bundlr.
Decentralized Identity (DID): Use Ethereum wallets (e.g., MetaMask) or identity protocols like ENS and Ceramic.
Community Moderation: Implement upvote/downvote systems, and explore DAO-style moderation.
Content Integrity: Let users cryptographically sign their comments for authenticity and verifiability.
