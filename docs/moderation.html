üéØ Our Moderation Philosophy

Commentator believes in community-driven moderation where users collectively decide what content is appropriate, rather than centralized censorship by platform owners.

Community-First

Users vote on content quality and appropriateness through democratic voting mechanisms.

Transparent Rules

Clear, public guidelines that apply equally to everyone, with transparent enforcement.

Protecting Users

Zero tolerance for harassment, doxxing, or content that endangers user safety.

DAO Governance

Decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) for community governance and rule evolution.

üìã Community Guidelines

‚úÖ What's Encouraged

  • Constructive feedback - Helpful reviews and honest opinions
  • Factual information - Accurate information with sources when possible
  • Respectful debate - Disagreement is fine, personal attacks are not
  • Helpful warnings - Alert others to scams, fraud, or security issues
  • Quality content - Well-written, thoughtful contributions

‚ùå What's Not Allowed

  • Spam - Repetitive, low-quality, or promotional content
  • Harassment - Personal attacks, threats, or targeted abuse
  • Doxxing - Sharing private personal information without consent
  • Illegal content - Content that violates applicable laws
  • Hate speech - Content targeting groups based on identity
  • Misinformation - Deliberately false information presented as fact

üó≥Ô∏è Community Voting System

Upvote/Downvote

Rate comment quality and relevance. High-quality content rises to the top through community consensus.

Content Reporting

Report content that violates guidelines. Community votes on whether reported content should be removed.

Reputation System

Build reputation through positive contributions. Higher reputation users have more influence in moderation decisions.

Automated Filtering

Heavily downvoted content is automatically hidden or requires user action to view.

üèõÔ∏è DAO Governance (Future)

Commentator will evolve into a fully decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) where the community governs the platform:

Governance Tokens

Earn governance tokens through positive contributions to the platform. Tokens grant voting rights on platform decisions.

Proposal System

Community members can propose changes to guidelines, features, or platform governance through formal proposals.

Elected Moderators

Community elects moderators for initial content review and appeals process with term limits and accountability.

Economic Incentives

Token rewards for quality contributions, successful moderation decisions, and platform improvement activities.

‚öñÔ∏è Enforcement Process

1

Community Detection

Users report problematic content or automated systems flag potential violations based on community voting patterns.

2

Community Review

Multiple community members review reported content and vote on whether it violates guidelines.

3

Transparent Action

If consensus is reached, appropriate action is taken (warning, content hiding, or account restrictions) with full transparency.

4

Appeals Process

Users can appeal decisions through a separate community review process with different reviewers.
